Our Mission

We are a passionate Venezuelan robotics team, national winners for the past two years, proudly representing our country in the World Robot Olympiad (WRO). Our mission is to inspire innovation, teamwork, and STEM excellence among youth, while breaking barriers to compete globally. This year, we aim to reach the WRO Open Championship in Panama, October 2025, and showcase the talent and resilience of Venezuelan students on the world stage.

About the Competition

The WRO is a global robotics competition that brings together thousands of students from over 85 countries to solve real-world challenges using robotics and creative engineering. We compete in the RoboSports category, where teams design, build, and program robots to play dynamic sports games, testing both technical skill and strategic thinking. Our journey to Panama is not just about winning—it's about representing Venezuela, sharing our story, and connecting with the global robotics community.

Global & National Impact

By participating in the WRO, we help put Venezuelan youth on the map, inspiring future generations to pursue science and technology. Our achievements have motivated local schools and communities, and we hope to continue making a positive impact both nationally and internationally. Supporting our team means investing in the future of STEM in Venezuela.
